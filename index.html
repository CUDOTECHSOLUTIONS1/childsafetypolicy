Alys: Meet Chat Date – Child Safety Standards

Purpose

Alys: Meet Chat Date is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. This includes implementing strict policies to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Our app is designed for users aged 18 and over, and we take proactive measures to protect minors from accessing our services.

Age Restrictions

  • Users under the age of 18 are not allowed to create accounts or access the app.
  • We use age verification during account registration to block minor users.
  • Google Play age restrictions and safeguards are strictly enforced within the app.

Reporting and Moderation

  • Users can report any child safety concerns directly in-app using our report feature.
  • All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team.
  • We remove content or suspend accounts that violate our child safety policies.

Collaboration with Authorities

Alys complies with all relevant child protection laws. Reports of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) are promptly escalated to regional and national authorities as required by law.

Preventive Measures

  • We do not allow minors to use our services.
  • We monitor and moderate all user-generated content to detect inappropriate behavior.
  • Our team actively works to detect and prevent the sharing of CSAM within the app.
  • We maintain robust technical and procedural safeguards to protect minors.
